The Best Movies according to Alberto

My Top 4 Best Movies of all time

Seven

One of the best detective stories writen for a movie. With two magnificent actors like Morgan Freeman and Brad Pitt and a great eerie atmosphere.
The end of this movie is simply shocking and it will be one of the best plot-twist of the cinema history.

Alien

What can we say about this film. The masterpiece of the Science Fiction writen by Ridley Scott that put the cinema industry upside down and I always recommend. Watch out with the alien!

Pride and Prejudice

If you love the love stories, this is, definitively, one of the best ones. With a great soundtrack that goes with the adventures that our two main characters have to get through.

Blade Runner

This is another great title borned in the 80s that makes the difference in terms of Science Fiction. Harrison Ford is this main character and the ashtonished soundtrack made by Vangelis makes this film unmatched.
